The Issue

A concern for physicans is how to optimize patient and practice management. What works and what doesn't? Physicans are looking for a tool to collect the motivation behind patients' actions, in order to better serve them and retain them as patients.

The Feedback

Paitents want support, but most of all they want real-time communition with their healthcare professionals, especially when things go wrong. They want to give feedback that will not go unanswered.

The Solution

Glimpse is helping to provide targeted feedback to physicans and pharmacists. Patients are asked the right questions at the right time. Openiing up the channel of communication between a physican and their patients. Using data-driven patient feedback, a doctor is now on course to provide better care.
