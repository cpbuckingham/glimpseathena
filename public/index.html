The Problem

A huge concern for doctors is losing patients through misdiagnosis. A patient may leave a doctor and go to another seeking a second opinion without the knowledge of the doctor. Resulting in a lost patient and a repeatable misdiagnoses.

The Feeback

Paitents want support, but most of all they want real-time communition with their healthcare professional. They want to give feeback that will not go unanswered.

The Solution

Glimpse is helping to close the gap of misdiagnosis and open up the channel of communication between a doctor and their patients. Using data-driven patient feedback, a doctor is now on course to provide better care.
